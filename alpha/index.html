How I do it? I know that simple, clear and fast user task completion brings joy and happiness. He knows that great things come in iterations.

I research for, design, improve and support digital products. [My methodology](https://www.notion.so/Ihor-Kosyriev-Digital-Product-Designer-f6999b05bbf34e97865488eb257b26f6) is based on current best practicies, and it covers all of the product cycle: research, planning, blueprint, design and testing, and support after a product or a solution to a problem was implemented.

I prefer **problem-first approach**, validated by **qualitative and quantitative research**, and **iterative design** with developers involved from the early stages.

I pay special attention to **problem discovery and validation**, **flowcharts**, **design systems** and **documentation**.
